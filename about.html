Our Story

Over a Decade of Digital Excellence

Founded in 2012 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, Seawind Solution started with a simple mission — to help businesses build a powerful digital presence. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service digital agency trusted by 3,500+ clients across the globe.

From responsive websites and mobile apps to AI-driven marketing and enterprise hosting, we deliver end-to-end solutions that drive real results. Our team of 65+ developers, designers, and strategists work with one goal: your success.
